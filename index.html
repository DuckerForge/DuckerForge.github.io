About

Who we are

DuckerForge is an independent developer publishing apps on the Solana Mobile dApp Store for the Seeker device. We focus on fast, native, self-custodial experiences.

Our apps are non-custodial: they connect through the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter and never hold user funds. Crypto is volatile and risky — our trading apps are tools, not financial advice.
